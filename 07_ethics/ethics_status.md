# Ethics status

## Approval

The departmental ethics committee determined that this project **did not require formal ethics approval**. Data collection involved healthy adult volunteers performing ordinary, non-strenuous trunk movements (bending, standing, controlled flexion) while wearing non-invasive surface sensors (skin-mounted IMUs and surface EMG electrodes), with no clinical intervention, no vulnerable participants, and no collection of sensitive personal data beyond the movement recordings.

## Participant materials

`Participant_Pack.docx` is the **blank** information-and-consent pack given to participants before each session. It covers what the study involved, voluntary participation, the right to withdraw, and how recordings would be used. It is included here as the governance record of what participants were told.

## What is deliberately excluded

- **Signed consent forms**: not included (participant privacy). No identifying participant documents are in this repository.
- **Raw on-body recordings**: excluded; all real participant data stays out of this repo. Only protocol-derived, anonymised aggregate results (per-participant AUCs labelled P01–P09) and synthetic data appear here.
